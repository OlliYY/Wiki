👨‍🏫 Instructions for Supervisors

This page is intended for Frostbit and Lapland UAS supervisors. It compiles general practices and tips for supervising interns.

📋 General Guidelines for Supervision

  • Kick-off meeting: held at the beginning of the internship (Day 1 or 2)
  • Weekly meeting: recommended once per week (15–30 min)
  • Worktime tracking: monitored through the intern's Excel document
  • Feedback: give clear and regular feedback (also written, if needed)
  • Responsible tasks: assign appropriately challenging tasks to the intern – not just assistant work

✅ Intern’s Responsibilities

  • Log daily working hours
  • Report progress weekly
  • Participate in meetings prepared
  • Take responsibility and document their own work
  • Be proactive and ask questions when needed

🚗 Booking a Car for the Intern

Interns can book a Lapland UAS car for project use if needed. The booking instructions may be unclear on the intranet, so follow the process below:

  1. First, as a supervisor, assess whether the car is necessary and suitable for the intended use.
  2. Book the car yourself via the university’s booking system (Lapland UAS – intranet → Operational Services → Car Booking).
  3. Enter the intern’s name and contact details as the user.
  4. Be responsible for any keys and returns yourself.

📌 Tip: The university's booking system is clearer than the intranet form – use it if possible.
